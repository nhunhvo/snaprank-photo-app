## ğŸ® User Actions & Flows

### Upload a Photo
1. Click "Upload" in navigation
2. Choose "Upload from Camera Roll" or "Take Photo"
3. Select/capture image
4. Choose category from dropdown
5. Click "Upload Photo"
6. Redirects to home, photo is now live

### Vote on Photos
1. Navigate to any category
2. Click thumbs up (like) or thumbs down (dislike)
3. Click again to remove vote
4. Switch votes by clicking the other button
5. Score updates immediately

### Create a Category
**Public:**
1. Go to Home page
2. Scroll to "Community Categories"
3. Click "Create Category"
4. Enter name, select "Public"
5. Click "Create Category"

**Private:**
1. Go to Home page
2. Scroll to "Private Categories" section
3. Click "Create Category"
4. Enter name, select "Private"
5. Click "Create Category"
6. Note the share code displayed
7. Share code with friends

### Join Private Category
1. Get share code from category creator
2. Go to Home page
3. Find "Private Categories" section
4. Click "Join with Code"
5. Enter share code
6. Click "Join Category"
7. Category now appears in your list

### Manage Badges
1. Go to Profile page
2. Click "Manage Badges"
3. Select up to 5 badges to display
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Badges appear on your profile

### Archive/Delete Photos
1. Go to Profile page
2. Hover over your photo
3. Click "Archive" to hide from public
4. Or click "Delete" to remove permanently
5. Toggle "Show Archived" to view archived photos
